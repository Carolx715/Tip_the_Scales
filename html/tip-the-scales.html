Tip The Scales

The four most common types of scales used in western music are:

Major, Natural Minor, Harmonic Minor, and Melodic Minor.

Can't tell the difference between these scales? No worries! - this audio classification program is trained to do it for you. It is able to distinguish between these four different types of common scales being played.
